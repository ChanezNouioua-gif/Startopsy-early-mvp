Common Questions

Everything you need to know about Startopsy

How is this different from talking to a mentor?

Mentors give subjective advice based on their experience. We analyze against real failure patterns with data-driven insights. You get objective analysis based on what actually killed similar startups.

What if my idea is very niche or unique?

Perfect! We look at similar failure patterns, not identical ideas. Every startup shares common risk factors (market validation, pricing, distribution, etc.) regardless of the niche.

Is the analysis really free?

Yes! We offer 10 free manual expert analyses per week. After that, it costs $12. The automated version (coming soon) will always be free.

What's included in the coaching program?

$29/month gets you: weekly check-ins, templates (pitch deck, financial model, GTM strategy), direct access to ask questions, and a small community of founders. Only available if your verdict is GO or PIVOT.

How long does the analysis take?

You'll receive your complete manual analysis within 24 hours. It's worth the wait - this is a detailed expert review, not an automated template.
